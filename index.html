4Cs of Diamond

Cut

Cut controls how a diamond sparkles. Precision, symmetry and polish define its brilliance.

Clarity

Clarity shows internal flaws. Grades: FL, IF, VVS, VS, SI, I.

Color

Color ranges from D (best) to Z. D-F colorless, G-J near colorless.

Carat

Carat is weight. Bigger carat doesn't always mean bigger look.

Important Disclaimer

Diamond grading may vary depending on testing conditions, equipment and methodology used. Color and clarity grades are based on gemological standards.

This verification system is for customer awareness and trust. Final grading is subject to COH Gemological policies.
